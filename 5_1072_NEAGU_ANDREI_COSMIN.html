How to play the puzzle game?

  • Upload the photo you want to have in the puzzle into the box located in the top right corner of the web page.
  • There is a default picture with two dogs. Drag and drop the one you wish to play the puzzle on.
  • Change the difficulty accordingly.
  • Shuffle the puzzle to get started.
  • Press 'Try again' if you want to return to the initial state.

Can't solve it yourself? No problem, hit the following button to watch the puzzle solving process!
